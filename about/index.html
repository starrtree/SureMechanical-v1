Who we are

A full-service HVAC and mechanical construction group

SURE Group brings together three specialized organizations under one standard for quality, safety, responsiveness, and technical execution.

Why SURE Group Exists

Owners and facility leaders need a partner that can design, build, fabricate, service, maintain, and manufacture specialty environments without losing accountability between scopes.

Mission

SURE Group delivers high-performance HVAC and mechanical solutions through experienced project teams, precision engineering, and a diverse, skilled workforce. Across every job, we prioritize quality, safety, efficiency, and long-term value for the facilities and communities we serve.

Vision

To be the region’s most trusted partner for HVAC and mechanical excellence by combining legacy, innovation, inclusive growth, and reliable execution.

Leadership

Chukwuma “Chuma” Ekwueme, President

Chukwuma “Chuma” Ekwueme founded SURE Mechanical and leads SURE Group with a mechanical engineering background, a Master’s of Engineering from the University of Cincinnati, LEED AP credentials, and involvement with ASHRAE, ASME, and USGBC.

He is a lean construction enthusiast and an advocate for minority participation in technical construction fields.
